GET LOST
August 1th, 2019

Never ever though I would do something as crazy as I'm about to do.

I've been selling, marketing and recruiting for the last 18 years of my life. I'm done. Like DONE.
Don't get me wrong, I still like all those things, but don't feel the joy and fulfillment as I used to. I started programming back in 2017, when I was working as Account Manager in It-related recruitment company. I wanted to fully understand their business and the employees they were looking for. The language they used was PHP, so that was an easy call. I took the basics of PHP in Code Academy and after finishing, started html and css. I was too busy at the time and had to quit after a while.
Last spring (2019) I realized, that I wanted to study something. My work was "too easy" and didn't give me what I needed to stay motivated. I was working full time and full time studies wasn't an option. I found out, that Helsinki Open University had the basic course for Java programming, that was online course. I signed up and started my journey. In the future, programming was something I would do for living! Also, not working full time anymore!
